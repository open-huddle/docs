Welcome to Open Huddle — an open-source, self-hostable alternative to proprietary team collaboration suites.

{/* truncate */}

This is the first post on the project blog. It marks a milestone we think is worth writing down: the scaffold is in, the governance documents are in, the first RPC works end-to-end, and contributors can clone and build the project from scratch.

What's next? We're walking through nine numbered phases publicly — CI and supply chain, identity and data, messaging, realtime, events and search, observability, Kubernetes deploy, and compliance hardening. Each phase lands with a corresponding ADR and, where operator-facing, an operator doc.

If you're following along, watch the repository. If you want to contribute, the [development setup guide](/docs/contributors/development-setup) is the place to start.

More soon.
